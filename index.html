BLUE INTRODUCTION

BLUEj aims to give visibility to everyone; each number seeks to cover not only the different orientations and gender identities, but also to deal with the different fetishes and tastes related to eroticism.

BLUEj was also born with the idea of ​​giving a different approach when configuring a graphic product of this type. With the rise of digital media and due to this social trend towards the rapid consumption of new themes and aesthetics (especially with regard to the sexual sphere) and in order to offer different themes in each issue, an attempt has been made to present this project with the aim of facilitating the development of the following issues of the magazine, offering a visual system that is flexible enough to adapt to the following themes.

OUR BRAND

BLUEj is about exploring the extent to which design can provoke sensations, excite and make sex and its diversity visible outside of the negative connotations it has inherited. It will also be an instrument to publicize and recover an aesthetic that today has become almost obsolete, the pulp, due to the trend towards minimalism in design.

…MISSION / VISION

MISSION:Make the field of eroticism visible, including other identities and ways of understanding eroticism and its diversity. Recover the pulp style, modernizing it and making it part of contemporary visual culture. Offer a different and open product with the intention of long-term continuity.

VISION: To establish itself in the sector of erotic magazines, making it stand out among its competitors. That the identidas achieve a strong pregnance, consolidating its own visual imagery that manages to adapt to the different themes and interests of the moment. To be a safe and interesting space for the queer collective.

PRINT APPLICATIONS

In the magazine, we play with the grid in a flexible way to obtain different results and compositions.

…GRID

There is a maximum margin for the exterior areas to promote the effect that everything tends inwards. The beginning of each story will have a smaller margin than the rest of the pages to generate a hierarchy of content. The page number should be as small as possible to prevent the reader from focusing on which page they are going through. In the headers of the stories, being the smallest margin, the page numbers will almost always be hidden. The horizontal guides at the top are based on the sizes of the titles and subtitles so that large titles are indented, cutting off the top and hiding part of the words. Despite placing a text block from top to bottom margin, the text block will always be closer to the top. When pages have only text, it will be allowed to unbalance the compositions, leaving blank spaces in the composition, making it appear that text is missing or hidden. Typography, when changing size and shape, will vary in its line spacing. The base grid would take care that the lines of text are aligned. Based on these established rules, the layout should be carried out more freely, experimenting with the grid to generate other sensations. For this grid to work, the inner pages must be of a low grammage to prevent the part of the texts closest to the interior from being completely hidden.

WEB APPLICATIONS

For web applications, user experience and interactivity will be prioritized, as well as the advertising pages of pulp magazines will be used as an aesthetic reference, maintaining a structure of vertical and horizontal lines that will separate the different sections of the content.

MOTION & ANIMATION

The BLUEj animation will be present above all in the web interaction. BLUEj animation will be present mostly in web interaction. It must be a clear and consistent animation but not too protagonist.

…BLUE CURVE

This curve is inspired by the movement of dense fluids as they fall: a higher speed at the beginning and a progressively slower descent. This type of animation brings a more casual aspect to the identity and more "playful" while maintaining the visual concept of the graphic. The development of the animations will be based mainly on the contents appearing and sliding from above, in addition to the animation of the variable fonts, which will widen and shrink.
